How to Calculate Yourself

Profit Target (Phase 1): Account Size × 0.10

Max Drawdown: Account Size × 0.10
Daily Loss (2-Step): Previous Balance × 0.05

Drawdown from Peak Formula

Remember: drawdown is calculated from your HIGHEST balance, not starting.

Actual Drawdown % = (Peak - Current) / Peak × 100
Example: Start $50k → Peak $54k → Current $51k
Drawdown = ($54k - $51k) / $54k × 100 = 5.56%
