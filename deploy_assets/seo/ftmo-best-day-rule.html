FTMO Best Day Rule: Complete Guide

The best day rule is specific to 1-Step challenges and catches many traders off guard. Your most profitable trading day cannot exceed 50% of your total profit.

How It Works

If your best single trading day's profit is $3,000, you need total profit of at least $6,000 before you can pass. This means one exceptional day won't let you skip past the challenge.

Example: You have one amazing day earning $4,000. To pass, you need total profit of at least $8,000 (2 × $4,000). You can't pass with $5,000 total even though it exceeds the 10% target.

Why FTMO Has This Rule

The best day rule prevents traders from passing with a single lucky trade. FTMO wants to see consistent profitability, not a one-time win.

How to Manage It

  • Track your best day as a percentage of total profit
  • If best day exceeds 40%, reduce position sizes
  • Keep trading until total profit is at least 2× your best day
