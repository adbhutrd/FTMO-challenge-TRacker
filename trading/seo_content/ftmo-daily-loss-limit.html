FTMO Daily Loss Limit: Complete Guide

The daily loss limit is one of the most strictly enforced FTMO rules. Going over it means instant challenge failure — even if you're profitable overall.

Daily Loss Limits by Challenge

Challenge TypeDaily Loss LimitOn $50k Account
2-Step Challenge5%$2,500/day
1-Step Challenge3%$1,500/day

How It's Calculated

The daily loss limit is calculated from the previous day's ending balance, not your starting balance.

Example: After a good day, your balance is $53,000. The next day's loss limit is 5% of $53,000 = $2,650 for 2-Step. You cannot lose more than this in a single day.

Common Mistakes

  • Using starting balance: The limit changes as your balance changes
  • Not accounting for open trades: Unrealized losses count toward the daily limit
  • Ignoring the reset: The limit resets each day based on the new balance
