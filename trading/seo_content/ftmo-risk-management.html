Recommended Risk Per Trade

  • Conservative: 0.5% per trade — recommended for beginners
  • Moderate: 1% per trade — suitable for experienced traders
  • Aggressive: 2% per trade — only for expert traders

Daily Loss Management

Never risk more than 50% of your daily loss limit in a single trade. This gives you room for 2-3 losing trades before hitting the limit.

Rule of thumb: If you lose 60% of your daily loss limit, stop trading for the day. Come back tomorrow fresh.
