FTMO Challenge for Beginners: Step-by-Step Guide

Taking your first FTMO challenge can be overwhelming. This guide walks you through everything from account selection to getting funded.

Step 1: Choose Your Challenge Type

Start with the 2-Step Challenge. It has more forgiving rules (5% daily loss limit) and no best day rule, making it better for beginners.

Step 2: Pick Your Account Size

Beginners should start with $10,000 or $25,000. The fees are lower (€155-€255) and the profit targets are more achievable while you learn the process.

Step 3: Learn the Rules

Before you trade, understand every rule:

  • Profit target: 10% Phase 1, 5% Phase 2
  • Max drawdown: 10% from peak balance
  • Daily loss limit: 5% (based on previous day close)
  • Minimum trading days: 4 per phase

Use our free FTMO tracker to monitor all rules automatically.

Step 4: Create Your Trading Plan

  • Max risk per trade: 0.5% of account
  • Daily profit target: 0.5-1%
  • Stop trading after 2 consecutive losses
  • Minimum 4 trading days per phase

Step 5: Start Trading

Begin with small positions. Your goal in the first week is to build a 2-3% profit buffer. This gives you room to handle drawdown later.

Step 6: Track Everything

After each trading day, log your ending balance in the free tracker. It automatically calculates P&L, drawdown, and progress.

Step 7: Pass and Get Funded

Once you hit your profit target and meet all requirements, FTMO verifies your challenge (24-48 hours) and you receive your funded account.

Beginner Tip: 60% of FTMO challenges fail from rule violations, not trading losses. Track your rules and you're already ahead of most traders.
