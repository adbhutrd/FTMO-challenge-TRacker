FTMO Trading Strategies That Actually Work

Not all trading strategies are suitable for FTMO challenges. Here are the approaches that consistently help traders pass.

1. Trend Following (Most Reliable)

Trend following is the most popular strategy among successful FTMO traders. Why? Because it has defined risk parameters and doesn't require constant monitoring.

  • Timeframe: 1H-4H charts
  • Entry: Pullback to moving average (20 EMA or 50 SMA)
  • Stop loss: Below recent swing low/high
  • Take profit: 2-3x risk (RR ratio)
  • Daily risk: Max 0.5% of account per trade

2. Breakout Trading (Good for Phase 2)

Breakout strategies work well when you need quick results in Phase 2's shorter target.

  • Key levels: Support/resistance from higher timeframes
  • Entry: Candle close above/below level
  • Stop loss: Inside the range
  • Volume confirmation: Higher volume on breakout

3. Scalping (for Experienced Traders)

Scalping can work for FTMO if you're disciplined about daily loss limits.

  • Timeframe: 1-5 minute charts
  • Max daily trades: 5-10
  • Stop loss: Always use tight stops
  • Daily profit target: 0.5% — stop after reaching it
