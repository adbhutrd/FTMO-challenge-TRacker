Phase 1: 10% Target

In Phase 1, you need to achieve a 10% profit on your starting balance while respecting the maximum drawdown limit (10%) and daily loss limits.

  • You can't have any daily loss exceeding the limit
  • 2-Step requires minimum 4 trading days
  • 1-Step has the best day rule (no single day > 50% of total profit)

Phase 2: 5% Target (2-Step Only)

After passing Phase 1, you enter Phase 2 with a reduced target of 5%. Your account resets to the starting balance, but the same drawdown rules apply.
