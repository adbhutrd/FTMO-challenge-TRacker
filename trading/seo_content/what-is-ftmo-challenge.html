What Is an FTMO Challenge? Complete Guide

FTMO is a prop trading firm that gives traders the opportunity to trade with funded accounts after passing an evaluation challenge.

How FTMO Works

  1. Choose a challenge: 1-Step or 2-Step
  2. Pay a fee: Ranges from €155 (10k) to €1,080 (200k)
  3. Trade to targets: Hit profit targets while respecting rules
  4. Get verified: Your trades are reviewed
  5. Get funded: Trade with FTMO's capital, keep up to 90% of profits

Key Rules

  • Profit target: 10% (Phase 1), 5% (Phase 2)
  • Max drawdown: 10% from peak balance
  • Daily loss limit: 5% (2-Step) or 3% (1-Step)
  • Minimum 4 trading days (2-Step)
  • No best day rule (2-Step) — applies for 1-Step
