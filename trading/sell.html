Yes, completely free forever. The free version includes the full FTMO rules engine, trade log, equity chart, and all challenge types. No signup, no account, no credit card. Pro adds cloud sync, unlimited accounts, PDF reports, and email alerts.

Do I need to create an account?

No! The web tracker stores everything in your browser's local storage. The Telegram bot also needs no account. Your data never leaves your devices unless you choose to upgrade to Pro for cloud sync.

Does it work with 1-Step challenges?

Yes, full support for both 1-Step and 2-Step FTMO challenges, including the 1-Step Best Day Rule (max 50% of total profit from a single day).

What if I lose my data?

Your data is stored in your browser's local storage. You can export it as JSON anytime. Pro users get automatic cloud sync so you never lose anything.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes! The web tracker works on any device with a browser. Plus, the Telegram bot (@ArdTradingBot) lets you track everything from your phone — add trades, check status, generate charts.
