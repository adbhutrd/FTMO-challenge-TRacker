## ❓ FAQ Section

**Q: Do you test for OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities?**
A: Yes! The OWASP Top 10 is the foundation of every test I perform. This includes XSS, SQL Injection, CSRF, SSRF, IDOR, authentication bypasses, and more. Every finding is manually verified.

**Q: What do I receive at the end?**
A: A professional PDF report containing: executive summary, detailed technical findings with proof-of-concept evidence, CVSS 3.1 severity ratings, and clear step-by-step remediation instructions for each vulnerability.

**Q: Is this legal? Do I need authorization?**
A: Yes, I require written authorization from the domain/application owner before testing begins. Please confirm you own the target or have written permission. I do not perform unauthorized testing.

**Q: Can you test a mobile app or API?**
A: Yes, I specialize in API security testing (REST, GraphQL). For mobile apps, please contact me first to discuss scope. The Standard and Premium packages include API testing.

**Q: How long does it take?**
A: Basic: 24 hours. Standard: 48 hours. Premium: 72 hours. I always communicate progress and can accommodate rush requests.

**Q: Will my live site be affected?**
A: I take extreme care to avoid disrupting your service. Tests are performed with rate limiting and safe payloads. For production systems, I recommend scheduling the test during low-traffic hours.

**Q: Do you provide a sample report?**
A: Yes, contact me and I'll share an anonymized sample report so you can see the quality before ordering.

**Q: What if you don't find anything?**
A: That's extremely rare, but I do a thorough job regardless. The report will document all tested areas and confirm your application's security posture.

**Q: Can I see a sample report before ordering?**
A: Yes! Contact me and I'll share an anonymized sample report so you can see the format, depth, and quality before purchasing.

**Q: Can you help fix the vulnerabilities?**
A: The report includes detailed remediation instructions. If you need hands-on help implementing fixes, I offer a separate consulting package — contact me to discuss.
