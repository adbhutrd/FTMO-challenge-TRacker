## 🎯 Best Practices for Proposals

1. **Customize every proposal** — Reference something specific from their job post
2. **Be first** — Apply within the first hour of a job being posted
3. **Keep it concise** — 3-4 paragraphs max
4. **Show proof** — "I found an XSS vulnerability in a similar app last week" (without breaking NDA)
5. **Price confidently** — Don't underprice. $50-100/hr is standard for pentesting
6. **Ask one question** — End with a question to start a conversation
7. **Follow up** — If they view your proposal but don't respond, send a polite follow-up after 48 hours
